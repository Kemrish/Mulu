About the agency

Professional domestic worker placement support

Mulu Employment Agency provides information about its housemaid placement services for households in foreign countries. The agency supports the offline recruitment and placement process from initial registration through household selection and travel processing.

This website is a portfolio and information page only. Housemaids do not upload passports, photos, or personal documents through the website. All document handling and placement steps are managed directly by the agency office.

Services

What Mulu Employment Agency does

01

Housemaid Registration

The agency receives housemaids at the office and begins the registration process with required identity details, passport, and photo records.

02

Household Matching

Suitable housemaids are presented for household selection based on the placement need, role expectations, and destination requirements.

03

Overseas Placement

Once selected, the agency coordinates the required offline processing before sending the housemaid to the household in the foreign country.

Placement process

Clear steps handled by the office

1

Initial office registration

The housemaid visits the agency office. The agency records the passport and takes a photo for internal placement documentation.

2

Household selection

Households review available candidates through the agency process and select the housemaid who fits their needs.

3

Agency processing

After selection, Mulu Employment Agency manages the required processing directly through the office.

4

Travel and placement

The housemaid is sent to the household abroad after the agency completes the placement requirements.
